About Me

I am a final year undergraduate student at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee majoring in Mechanical Engineering. Since my first year, I have involved myself in diverse activities starting from developing Apps in Android to Robotics to being a mechanical team executive at the Formula Student team of my institute. I am quite passionate about starting my career in the fields of robotics which is a perfect combination of my Mechanical Engineering knowledge and Programming vehemence.

Being a member of a Formula Student team, I am quite captivated by the autonomous driverless systems. The interesting concepts of localization, mapping and navigation entice and motivate me to come up with state-of-the-art solutions.

Recently, I started exploring Reinforcement learning strategies which intrigued me with its wide variety of applications in learning from known or unknown situations. I really like the concept as it directly correlates with how we, as humans, think and act.

Apart from my professional and work life, I have many extra-curricular interests as well. I meet a lot of people and make great friends. You can find more by visiting my ME Page

Internships and Collaborations

May 2020 - Present

Collaborator, EPFL Biorobotics Laboratory

I am currently involved in a project with Dr. Dimitar Stanev under Dr. Auke Ijspeert's Biorobotics Lab at EPFL. The work deals with developing reinforcement learning agents efficient enough to make a musculo-skeletal human model do the desired tasks. I learnt to use Opensim, Simbody and executing SLURM cluster jobs.

May 2020 - Present

Collaborator, Nantes Laboratory of Digital Sciences

I am doing a project under the guidance of Dr. Stephane Caro, Head of Team RoMaS at LS2N. The project deals with path planning of a Cable Driven Parallel Robot in complex obstacle-cluttered environments under certain objectives. The project is on MATLAB.

April 2020 - Present

Reinforcement Learning Intern, Swaayatt Robots

I am involved as an intern with the Indian Self-Driving startup. My work was dedicated towards developing reinforcement learning based behavioral planner for autonomous driving. The agents were simulated in the CARLA Self Driving Simulator coupled with CARLA-ROS Bridge to use all the ROS functionalities.

May 2019 - July 2019

Undergraduate Research Fellow, SPARK IIT Roorkee

I was selected for the IIT Roorkee SPARK Summer Undergraduate Research Fellowship. I was supervised by Dr. Ankit Bansal to do a work based on statistical sampling in Monte Carlo estimates for Radiative Heat Transfer. The work was accepted and to be published by the ASME Summer Heat Transfer Conference 2020.
