Casual ME

I live in Kolkata, notorious as the City of Joy. You can think of me as a Bengali but my family originates from Bihar. I was born and brought up in here but my mother tongue is Hindi, so it is fine to call my language an imperfect but amusing mixture of both. I have a dangerous prediliction for the famous Bengali "Rosogolla". My life revolves around a small and sweet family of six comprising of my precious parents, grandparents and my dearest sister. Music captivates me and coming up with one of my own seems so exciting.

My college life at IIT Roorkee, my second home, have been so much fun where I experienced being on my own, travelling alone, go on trips and late night fun activities. This is the last year of my undergraduate life and I am quite nostalgic about me standing at the main gate of IIT Roorkee on my first day here.
